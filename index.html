AI Fraud Detection

Our intelligent system analyzes reports to identify and flag potential false information, ensuring police resources are used effectively.

Role-Based Access

A secure login system for citizens, officers, and administrators with specific permissions to protect sensitive data.

GPS Location Tagging

Automatically pinpoints the crime location using GPS for accurate and faster response from law enforcement.
